Karate

Our dominant style of karate is Wado-Ryu. This is an open hand, self defence style of karate, teaching discipline and effective self defence.

Kickboxing

American Kickboxing is a high paced, controlled style of combat. It well suits those looking to improve both technique and fitness.

Kung-Fu

The ancient art of Kung Fu is designed to develop both physical and mental fitness. Kung Fu is great for students with physical disabilities due to its slower and more controlled pace.

Fitness

Our fitness classes are influenced by our martial arts expertise and offer a fresh approach to working out.
